Thought Diary

When you feel bad, you sometimes wish you could snap your fingers and feel better. Unfortunately, that doesn't work. You can't force yourself to feel happy, no matter how much you want to. But your feelings are largely determined by your thoughts. If you can change your thoughts, and get rid of negative patterns of thinking, you can change how you feel. This thought diary will help you keep track of your thoughts, and challenge your negative thinking patterns. Use the Thought Diary to control these thoughts. Learn how to think more constructively and feel better.
